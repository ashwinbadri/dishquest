  # DishQuest Privacy Policy                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  Last updated: April 29, 2026
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  ## Information We Collect                                                                                                                                                                                    
   
  **Location**                                                                                                                                                                                                 
  DishQuest requests access to your device's location solely to find
  restaurants near you that serve the dish you're exploring. Location                                                                                                                                          
  is used in real time and is never stored or shared.                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  **No account required**                                                                                                                                                                                      
  DishQuest does not require you to create an account. We do not                                                                                                                                               
  collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  **Saved dishes**                                                                                                                                                                                             
  Dishes you save are stored locally on your device only. They are                                                                                                                                             
  never uploaded to our servers.                                                                                                                                                                               
   
  ## Third-Party Services                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                            
  - **Google Places API** — used to search nearby restaurants.                                                                                                                                                 
    Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
  - **Firebase** — used to serve dish data.                                                                                                                                                                    
    Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).                                                                                                                                 
  - **Spoonacular API** — used to fetch dish images and ingredients.
    Subject to [Spoonacular's Privacy Policy](https://spoonacular.com/privacy).                                                                                                                                
                                                            
  ## Data Sharing

  We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third                                                                                                                                                
  parties beyond the services listed above.
