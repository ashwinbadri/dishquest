DishQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Information We Collect

Location

DishQuest requests access to your device's location solely to find restaurants near you. Location is used in real time and is never stored or shared.

No account required

DishQuest does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.

Saved dishes

Dishes you save are stored locally on your device only and never uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Places API & Firebase — used to find restaurants and serve dish data.
  • Spoonacular API — used to fetch dish images and ingredients.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties beyond the services listed above.
